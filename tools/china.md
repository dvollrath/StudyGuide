# Chinese growth
We spent a lot of time discussing how and why China's growth rate was so high. To do that we used Gemini chats to analyse the data. The data is from the [Penn World Tables](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/6rrc8kizipa9zjrgmprph/pwt110.csv?rlkey=h3zonx7jiw0hpqffk1mj2442p&st=t4xud953&dl=1) which you would need to replicate the analysis.
